Alaba Rago Transnational Market Redeveloped with LAGIS
Alaba Rago Transnationa; Market Limited, uses GIS to identify and analyze land use needs and environmental design for for the redvelopment of Alaba International market in Ojo Alaba, Lagos.. Planners use geospatial tools to consider multiple site suitability factors such as slope, tree cover, and solar radiation, and to calculate the expected energy production of proposed projects.

Mapping the regional conflict zones around Africa
Conflicts around Africa have caused widespread displacement, migration, poverty and diseases. We have partnered with Open Migration Africa to map conflict zones in Africa, where they are happening and the resultant effects on people and the environment.

JLL delivers intelligence to thousands of employees
JLL, one of the world's largest real estate firms partners with I-AGIS to equip over 3,000 employees with geospatial data to unlock market intelligence and guide strategic decisions.
